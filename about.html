ABOUT

Morgenshtern is a Russian rapper, singer, and music producer known for his provocative style and controversial lyrics. He has gained significant popularity in Russia and internationally.

Morgenshtern's music often blends elements of hip-hop, rap, and pop, creating a unique sound that resonates with a wide audience. He is also known for his extravagant music videos and public persona.
